Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

We offer a hassle-free return policy within 30 days of purchase. The shoes must be in their original condition, unworn, and with all the original packaging. Please refer to our Returns and Exchanges page for detailed instructions.

To find the perfect fit, we recommend using our shoe size chart available on our website. It provides accurate measurements to help you select the right size. Additionally, you can refer to our fitting guide for tips on measuring your feet for an optimal fit.

Yes, we offer international shipping to many countries. During the checkout process, you can select your country from the available options. Please note that additional customs fees and import duties may apply, which are the responsibility of the customer.

We are committed to sustainability, and many of our shoe models are made with eco-friendly materials. Check the product descriptions on our website for information on the materials used. We strive to minimize our environmental impact while maintaining high-quality standards.

Proper care ensures the longevity of your shoes. We recommend following the care instructions specific to each shoe model, as provided on our website or included with your purchase. Generally, most shoes can be cleaned with a soft cloth or brush and mild detergent. Avoid using harsh chemicals or machine washing unless explicitly stated.

Yes, once your order is processed and shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track the progress of your shipment on our website or the shipping carrier's website.

We accept various payment methods, including major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and Apple Pay. During the checkout process, you can choose the option that suits you best.

We occasionally offer discounts and promotions. Stay updated by subscribing to our newsletter or following us on social media platforms. Keep an eye out for special sales events, holiday promotions, or exclusive offers.

We strive to process orders promptly; however, if you need to cancel or modify your order, please contact our customer support as soon as possible. We will do our best to accommodate your request, but we cannot guarantee changes if the order has already been shipped.

You can reach our customer support team by pressing the button below. We are available during business hours to assist you with any inquiries or concerns you may have.
